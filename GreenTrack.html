Green Freight: Your Trusted Partner for Sustainable Cargo Solutions

Welcome to Green Freight, your go-to destination for sustainable freight transportation solutions. We specialize in eco-friendly cargo transportation services that prioritize environmental responsibility without compromising on efficiency or reliability.

In Australia, we operate the Melbourne-Sydney rail corridor. We hope to increase the amount of freight delivered between the two largest Australian Cities by rail, improving reliability, sustainability and worker safety. Improving connections is vital as in recent years only 2% of freight between Melbourne and Sydney goes by rail.

In the UK, we operate the West Coast Main Line and freight services through the channel tunnel to the EU. We hope to improve UK's rail connections to the EU to further increase trade, cooperation and sustainable development. Our UK fleet is fully electric.
